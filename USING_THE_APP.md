# How to Use Lead Tracker

## Overview

Lead Tracker has 4 main sections:

1. **Dashboard** - See your leads at a glance
2. **All Leads** - Browse & manage your lead database
3. **Add Lead** - Quick entry for new prospects
4. **Analytics** - Understand your lead pipeline

---

## Dashboard Tab

### What You See

**Key Metrics (Top of page):**
- **Total Leads** - Count of all leads in your system
- **Hot Leads** - Number of promising prospects ready to close
- **Due Today** - Leads that need follow-up today
- **Hot Lead Rate** - % of leads that are hot (aim for 30%+)

**Status Distribution Chart**
- Shows breakdown of Hot/Warm/Cold leads
- Red = Hot (ready to close)
- Orange = Warm (needs nurturing)
- Blue = Cold (early stage)

**Top Lead Sources Chart**
- Shows which channels bring the most leads
- Helps you optimize marketing spend
- Example: "Google Ads brings 8 leads, Referrals bring 5"

**Upcoming Follow-ups Table**
- Lists leads that need contact in next 7 days
- Shows name, email, phone, status, follow-up date
- Sorted by date (most urgent first)

### How to Use It

1. **Check every morning** - See what's due today
2. **Spot patterns** - Which sources work best?
3. **Track progress** - Is your hot lead % increasing?
4. **Plan your day** - Prioritize hot leads

---

## All Leads Tab

### What You See

Interactive table showing all your leads with columns:
- **Name** - Company name
- **Email** - Contact email
- **Phone** - Contact phone
- **Source** - Where lead came from
- **Score** - Quality score (0-100)
- **Status** - Hot/Warm/Cold
- **Last Contact** - When you last reached out
- **Next Follow-up** - When to contact again

### How to Use It

**Search for a lead:**
- Type in the search box (top right)
- Filters across all columns in real-time
- Example: Type "Google" to find all leads from Google Ads

**Sort a column:**
- Click column header to sort ascending/descending
- Click again to reverse order

**Page through results:**
- Use page selector at bottom (shows 20 leads per page)

**Export data:**
- Click CSV button to download all leads
- Use in Excel, Google Sheets, or email

**Identify action items:**
- Find leads where Status = "Hot" AND Last Contact > 7 days old
- These are at-risk deals needing attention

## Add Lead Tab

### Quick Entry Form

**Step 1: Enter Basic Info**
```
Company Name: [Acme Corp]
Email: [contact@acme.com]
Phone: [555-0101]
```
(Name and email are required; phone is optional)

**Step 2: Set Lead Details**
```
Lead Source: [Select from dropdown]
  Options: Google Ads, Referral, Website Form, 
           Cold Outreach, Social Media, Event, Other

Lead Score: [Use slider, 0-100]
  0-30 = Cold (early conversations)
  31-70 = Warm (showing interest)
  71+ = Hot (ready to buy)

Status: [Select Hot/Warm/Cold]
  Match this to your score, or override based on gut feel
```

**Step 3: Add Notes**
```
Notes: [Any details that matter]
  Example: "Budget approved", "Waiting on decision", 
           "Wants demo Thursday", etc.
```

**Step 4: Submit**
- Click blue "Add Lead" button
- Confirmation message appears
- Form clears automatically
- Lead is added to database

### What Happens Automatically

When you add a lead:
✅ System records today's date as "Last Contact"  
✅ Sets "Next Follow-up" to 2 days from today  
✅ Calculates based on your entered score  
✅ Lead appears in Dashboard & All Leads tab

## Analytics Tab

### Charts & Metrics

**Lead Score Distribution**
- Histogram showing how many leads at each quality level
- Tall bar on left = lots of cold leads (needs nurturing)
- Tall bar on right = lots of hot leads (good momentum)

**Conversion Metrics Box**
- Total Leads count
- % Hot / Warm / Cold
- Average lead score

**Lead Age vs Quality Scatter Plot**
- X-axis = Days since last contact
- Y-axis = Lead score
- Bubble size = how good the lead is
- **Red zone (upper left):** Hot leads that need follow-up urgently
- **Green zone (upper right):** You're nurturing well
- **Yellow zone (lower left):** Should follow up
- **Gray zone (lower right):** Low priority, can wait

### How to Use It

1. **Identify stale leads**
   - Look at right side of scatter plot
   - These haven't been contacted in days
   - Consider re-engaging them

2. **Check score trends**
   - Monitor histogram over time
   - More right = getting better at scoring
   - More center = lots of maybes (needs qualification)

3. **Optimize follow-ups**
   - See which leads are aging fastest
   - Adjust follow-up frequency accordingly

4. **Benchmark performance**
   - Take weekly screenshots
   - Track % hot leads week-over-week
   - Celebrate when it improves!

## Common Tasks

### "I have 10 new leads from a trade show"
1. Click Add Lead tab
2. Add each one (takes ~30 sec per lead)
3. Set source to "Event"
4. Estimate score based on conversation quality
5. Dashboard automatically tracks them

### "I just emailed a hot lead"
1. Go to All Leads tab
2. Find the lead (search by name)
3. Note today's date in your notes
4. Next session, mark next follow-up for 48 hours out

### "Which lead source is best?"
1. Go to Dashboard
2. Look at "Top Lead Sources" chart
3. Plan next month's marketing based on top performers
4. Consider reducing spend on low performers

### "I'm losing hot leads"
1. Go to Dashboard → Upcoming Follow-ups
2. Set phone reminders for each one
3. Follow up 24 hours before next_followup date
4. Move closed deals to CRM or separate folder

### "What's my conversion rate?"
1. Go to Analytics tab
2. Look at Conversion Metrics box
3. Hot % = your conversion rate
4. Aim for 25-35% hot leads in pipeline

### Scoring Guide

**What makes a lead "Hot" (score 75+)?**
- Budget confirmed
- Decision maker present
- Timeline aligned
- Clear business need

**What makes a lead "Warm" (score 40-74)?**
- Interested, but asking questions
- Checking with team
- Testing your solution
- Timeline unclear

**What makes a lead "Cold" (score 0-39)?**
- Early stage conversation
- Just collecting info
- Long timeline
- May not have budget

## Tips & Tricks

### Use Lead Score Strategically

Scoring isn't just math - it's a **sales tool**:
- Score represents your confidence this will close
- Use your gut + the criteria
- Adjust as you learn more about the lead

Example:
- Initial contact: score 50 (unknown)
- They download whitepaper: +10 points (engaged)
- You learn they have budget: +15 points (serious)
- They ask for a demo: +10 points (ready)
- Final score: 85 (hot!)

### Follow-up Timing

The "2-day default" is just a start:
- **Hot leads:** Follow up every 24-48 hours
- **Warm leads:** Follow up every 3-5 days
- **Cold leads:** Follow up every 1-2 weeks
- **Just closed:** Mark as "Won" (add feature in phase 2)

### Source Optimization

Track quality, not just quantity:

```
Source          Leads   Hot %   Avg Time to Close
Google Ads      45      35%     12 days     ← Best ROI!
Referral        8       62%     5 days      ← Best quality!
Website Form    12      20%     25 days     ← Needs work
Cold Outreach   5       40%     18 days
```

Use this data to decide where to invest.

## Troubleshooting

### "A lead isn't showing up"
- Check All Leads tab and search by name
- Make sure you clicked "Add Lead" button
- Try refreshing the page

### "My data disappeared"
- The app currently stores data in memory (resets on page refresh)
- Phase 2 will add database persistence
- For now, keep a backup CSV export

### "The chart looks wrong"
- Try refreshing the page
- Check that leads have scores assigned
- Ensure dates are valid

### "I need more fields"
- Current MVP has essential fields
- Phase 2 will add custom fields
- For now, use Notes field for extra info
